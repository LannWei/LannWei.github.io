About

I am a second-year MS student in the School of Computer Science and Technology at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), AIoT Group, supervised by Prof. Nikolaos M. Freris. (2021 ~ Present)

Prior to my MS, I received my Bachelor's degree from the Department of Computer Science at School of Informatics, Xiamen University (2017 ~ 2021). I spent an unforgettable winter in 2020 doing internship at Manteia Technologies, advised by Prof. Liansheng Wang.

My research interests are in model compression and physics-informed graph neural network, particularly Transformer pruning, learning-based fluid simulation. I also work on transformer-based segmentation tasks.
